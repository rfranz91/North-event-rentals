Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reserve rentals?

Submit the quote form with your event date, location and requested items. We’ll confirm availability and send the next steps.

Do you deliver and pick up?

Delivery and pickup options depend on the event location, order size and schedule. Any applicable fee will be included in your quote.

How far in advance should I book?

Book as early as possible, especially for weekends and popular dates. Last-minute requests may be accommodated when inventory is available.

Is a deposit required?

Reservation and payment terms will be provided with your quote and rental agreement.

What happens if it rains?

Outdoor rentals require a weather plan. Some items may need to be moved under cover or rescheduled depending on conditions.

Do you set everything up?

Setup options vary by rental. Your quote will clearly state what is included.
