Frequently Asked Questions

Answers Before You Rent

We've answered some of the questions we're asked most often. If you don't see your question here, we'd love to help.

Do I need to pay a deposit?

Yes. A deposit is required to reserve your rental items and event date. The remaining balance is due before delivery or pickup.

How far do you deliver?

We proudly serve Rockford and surrounding Northern Illinois communities. Delivery outside our normal service area may be available for an additional fee.

Do you set everything up?

Yes. Depending on the rental items, we can deliver, set up, and return afterward to pick everything up so you can enjoy your event.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking as early as possible, especially during wedding season and summer weekends, as popular items reserve quickly.

What happens if it rains?

Weather happens. We'll work with you to discuss available options and make every reasonable effort to accommodate changes whenever possible.

Can I pick up rentals myself?

Some smaller rental items may be available for customer pickup. Larger rentals generally require delivery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit cards, debit cards, cash, and other payment methods that will be listed during booking.

Do you offer package discounts?

Absolutely! Bundling multiple rental items together is often more affordable than renting each item individually.

What if an item gets damaged?

Normal wear is expected. Damage beyond normal use may result in repair or replacement charges as outlined in our rental agreement.

How do I request a quote?

Simply visit our Contact page and complete the quote request form. We typically respond within one business day.

Still Have Questions?

We're happy to help. Whether you're planning a wedding, graduation, birthday party or corporate event, we'll help you choose the perfect rentals.
