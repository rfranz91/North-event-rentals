About Northwood

A family business built around dependable service and memorable celebrations.

Mike & Ryan

From helping customers celebrate to helping bring the fun

People sometimes ask how two brothers with a combined 32 years in retail ended up in the event-rental business. The answer is simple: we’ve spent years helping people prepare for weddings, birthdays, graduations, anniversaries and every celebration in between.

Along the way, we learned that the best events are not just about what is being celebrated—they’re about the people, the laughter and the details that help everyone enjoy the day.

Northwood Event Rentals was created to provide fun, dependable rentals with personal service. We’re focused on making the rental side straightforward so you can focus on the celebration.
