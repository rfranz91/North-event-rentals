About Us

Built on Service. Driven by Celebration.

People often ask us, "How did two guys from a liquor store end up in the event rental business?"

After a combined 32 years in retail, we've helped thousands of customers prepare for weddings, birthdays, graduations, anniversaries, retirement parties, backyard barbecues, and just about every celebration you can imagine. Along the way, we realized that what we enjoyed most wasn't just helping people find what they needed—it was being a small part of their biggest moments.

That's what inspired us to create Northwood Event Rentals. We wanted to make hosting easy by providing clean, dependable rentals backed by the same friendly, reliable service we've built our careers on.

Whether you're planning an elegant wedding, a graduation party, a family reunion, or a casual backyard get-together, we're here to help you create an event your guests will remember.

From tables and chairs to yard games, décor, and unique event rentals, our goal is simple: Reliable Rentals. Memorable Events.
